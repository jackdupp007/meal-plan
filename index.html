Post-bowel surgery — important
Introduce high-fibre foods (lentils, beans) gradually — especially in Week 1.
Drink 2–2.5L water per day. Fibre without fluid causes discomfort.
Smaller, more frequent meals may suit you better than large ones.
If any meal causes bloating, swap it and note it in Journable.
Consider a bowel-focused dietitian consult — they can fine-tune the fibre approach for your resection.

Journable logging tips
Log before or during eating — not after. Keeps portions honest.
Log the latte first thing every morning to set the day's tone.
A day where you log everything and go slightly over beats a day you don't log at all.
Sync Garmin so active calories auto-adjust your daily budget.
