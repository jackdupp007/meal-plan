Woolworths tips
Tofu: Vitasoy or Evergreen firm tofu in the fridge section near produce. Buy 3 packs.
Oat milk: Oatly Barista or Minor Figures in the long-life milk aisle.
Vegan curry paste: Maesri green curry paste in Asian foods aisle — check label for no shrimp paste.
Estimated weekly cost: $110–$150 AUD depending on pantry stock.
Your plan at a glance
Calorie targets
Journable goal: 2,100 cal/day
Dog walk days: eat up to 2,300 cal — trust your Garmin.
Wednesday (Warhammer): lighter lunch banks ~1,000 cal for fast food.
Weekday latte (regular caramel soy) = 230 cal — already counted in every weekday budget.
Foods removed from this plan
No pumpkin, hummus, avocado, brown rice or quinoa anywhere in this plan.
Replaced with: basmati rice, jasmine rice, white potato, sweet potato, extra lentils and beans.
Cooking style this week
Mon–Fri dinners: all under 30 minutes. Mostly one-pan or one-pot.
Saturday dinner: fancier — allow 45–60 min. Worth it.
Sunday dinner: relaxed cook, doubles as batch prep for the week.
Sunday batch prep (40 min)
2 jars overnight oats for Mon & Thu.
8–10 date-and-oat bliss balls (blend oats, dates, peanut butter — roll and refrigerate).
Big pot of red lentil soup for lunches (you already know how good this is!).
Pot of basmati rice — keeps 4 days, speeds up every weeknight dinner.
Post-surgery reminders
Drink 2–2.5L water daily. Essential with high-fibre vegan eating.
If lentils or beans cause bloating, reduce portion size and build up gradually.
Smaller serves more often is fine — adjust snack sizes up if needed.
This plan is a guide, not a prescription.
Swap anything that doesn't work. Check with your GP for dietary changes given your surgery history.
