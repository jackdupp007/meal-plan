Shopping tips for Woolworths
Tofu: Woolworths stocks Vitasoy or Evergreen firm tofu in the fridge section near produce. Buy 3 packs for the week.
Oat milk: Oatly Barista or Minor Figures in the long-life milk aisle. Barista versions froth better for your latte habit at home on weekends.
Vegan curry paste: Check the Asian foods aisle for Maesri green curry paste — confirm vegan on label (no shrimp paste).
Medjool dates are in the dried fruit aisle — used for bliss ball prep on Sundays.
Frozen edamame is in the freezer section. Shelled is easiest — just microwave and salt.
Estimated weekly shop cost: roughly $120–$160 AUD depending on what you already have in the pantry.

Post-bowel surgery considerations
Introduce high-fibre foods (lentils, beans, bran) gradually. Start with smaller portions and build up over the first 2–3 weeks.
Drink 2–2.5L water per day. Fibre without fluid causes discomfort, especially post-resection.
Smaller, more frequent meals may suit you better than 3 large ones. Adjust snack sizes up if needed.
If any food causes bloating or discomfort, swap it out and mention it to your GP or dietitian.
Consider a consult with a bowel-focused dietitian — they can tailor the fibre approach specifically to your resection.
